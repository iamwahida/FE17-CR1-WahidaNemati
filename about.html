I'm glad you found your way here. I suppose you want to know more about us. Then you've come to the right place! 😄

The "Food Blog" page was founded in 2020 at the time of the Corona Pandemic. We were not allowed to go out to eat because the entire gastronomy was closed. That's why we created the page here, so that everyone from every kitchen can share their favourite recipes with the next person. This way we learn a lot and best of all, we enjoy the homemade food!
